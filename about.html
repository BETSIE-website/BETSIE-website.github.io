About Us

Climate change and the effects it is causing are accelerating, caused by an increase in greenhouse gas emissions. The world is getting alarmingly close to irreversible changes in major ecosystems and the planetary climate system. Exhausting Earth’s resources and contributing to climate change are not the only problems of the conventional meat industry. According to the People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA) companies are treating the animals in an inhumane manner cramping them together in very tiny spaces. As a result, animals get vaccinated to protect diseases and pathogens from spreading easily and infecting the livestock. Remnants of these vaccines or diseases are often present in the meat and therefore can affect human health.

"Therefore, it is time to change the conventional meat industry!"

Our solution for these problems is cultured meat, which is laboratory grown meat. In the production process of cultured meat, muscle cells and stem cells are obtained from animals under anesthesia to make sure no harm is caused. These animal cells are grown with nutrients that stimulate the muscle tissue to duplicate. Once enough cells are created, they are rearranged into fibers which in their turn are rearranged into structures that are similar to meat.
