Recipe: Chicken skewers

Home Other Recipes Chicken skewers

INGREDIENTS FOR 2 PERSONS

  • 400 grams of cultured chicken filets
  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tablespoon of sambal
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons of ketchup
  • Salt and pepper
  • Skewers
  • Bell pepper
  • Parsley
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

PREPARATIONS

  1. Finely chop the garlic and mix it with the sambal, ketchup, salt and pepper.
  2. Cut the cultured chicken filets in 2 cm pieces and mix the chicken pieces with sambal mixture.
  3. Marinade overnight.
  4. Slice up the onion and bell pepper in pieces of 2 cm.
  5. Alternately add the chicken, bell pepper and the onion on the skewers.
  6. Grill the chicken skewers for 7 minutes (rotate continuously)
  7. In the meanwhile mix mustard and mayonnaise 1:1, and put it in a small bowl.
  8. Take the satays out of the grill pan and place them on a plate.
  9. Finely chop the parsley and use it as garnish Voilá, your dinner is served.
