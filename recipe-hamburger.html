Recipe: Hamburger

Hamburger Blog Blog Single

Ingredients for 2 persons

  • 400 grams of cultured grinded meat
  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • Ketchup
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 teaspoons mustard
  • 1 rusk
  • 1 egg
  • A few drops of Tabasco sauce
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 buns (up to choice)
  • 2 pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Truffle mayonnaise

PREPERATIONS

  1. Finely chop the onion and put it in a big mixing bowl with the cultured grind beef, a dash of salt and pepper and one egg.
  2. Crumble the rusk into this mixture and also add the mustard, one tablespoon of ketchup and tabasco sauce. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Divide the mixture into two equal parts and form burgers.
  4. Pour oil into a grill pan and grill the burger for 12 minutes, flip every 2 minutes). Add the cheddar cheese after 10 minutes.
  5. In the meantime, slice open the buns and grill the open cut sides in a pan.
  6. Slice the lettuce, tomatoes, pickles to put on the burger.
  7. When all is done, put the buns on a plate, add the truffle mayonnaise, the burgers, the tomatoes, lettuce and pickles to the burger (add additional ketchup to your liking). Voil√°, your dinner is served!
